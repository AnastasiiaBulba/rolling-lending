About the Game

Rolling Sky Ball is a thrilling 3D ball game that tests your speed, timing, and musical reflexes! Guide your ball through a path full of traps and obstacles as you bounce to the beat of exciting music.

Swipe, dodge, and jump in perfect sync with the rhythm to stay on track. Feel the rush of speed and the thrill of near misses in this addictive and visually stunning game.
