FAQ

Is the game free to play?

Yes, Rolling Sky Ball is free with optional in-app purchases.

Is it available offline?

Yes, you can play offline, though some features require an internet connection.

What devices are supported?

Available on Android devices via Google Play.

Is the music original?

Yes, the game features a curated mix of original and themed tracks.

Can I customize my ball?

Unlock a variety of colorful balls as you progress.
